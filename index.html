     Mission Statement

    Nowadays, there is a great demand for software development out there. The world needs software solutions just about
    anything. From planning and running complex business and industrial services to planning and running your day. From
    execution of mission critical operations to playing for fun, almost everything is backed by a software. There are millions of
    software developers out there and yet the global need for them is not about to be met. The world needs a lot more
    software developers, but seriously, why do we need them, what is the mission of a software developer that is so important
    to the world economy?



    My mission as a developer is to further the availability of resources to those
    that do not have the same privilege of access as I do.
    I plan to create assets and programs that are accessible to everyone,
    whether they are tech literate or not.


    One day I would like to create a game development company and employ
    designers and developers that can use video games as their canvas.
    My mission is to always keep a positive and uplifting message in
    everything I create.


    Arabella McEntire
